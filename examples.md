# Sector Examples for Agentic AI Threats

## Healthcare and Digital Health

**Example Risk:** Data Exposure  
An AI assistant summarizes patient records and accidentally includes sensitive patient information in a message sent to an unauthorized user.

**Possible Controls:**  
- Patient data access restriction
- Human review
- Output filtering
- Audit trails
- Data protection review

---

## Finance, Banking, and Payment Systems

**Example Risk:** Tool Misuse  
An AI agent with access to payment workflow tools triggers or recommends an unauthorized transaction action.

**Possible Controls:**  
- Tool-use approval
- Least privilege
- Transaction approval workflow
- Fraud monitoring
- Audit logs

---

## Government and Public-Sector Digital Systems

**Example Risk:** Opaque Decision-Making  
An AI-supported system makes or recommends a citizen service decision without clear explanation or appeal pathway.

**Possible Controls:**  
- Decision logs
- Human review
- Appeal mechanism
- Governance policy
- Public accountability review

---

## Telecommunications and Data Infrastructure

**Example Risk:** Operational Disruption  
An AI automation tool changes a network configuration incorrectly and affects service availability.

**Possible Controls:**  
- Rollback controls
- Change approval
- Network monitoring
- Incident response
- Manual override

---

## Energy and Utilities

**Example Risk:** Excessive Autonomy  
An AI system recommends or triggers unsafe operational actions in an OT or ICS environment without proper human control.

**Possible Controls:**  
- Manual override
- OT access restriction
- Fail-safe procedure
- Red-teaming
- High-risk approval

---

## Transport and Logistics

**Example Risk:** Goal Misalignment  
An AI routing tool prioritizes speed and cost reduction while ignoring safety, compliance, or service reliability constraints.

**Possible Controls:**  
- Objective validation
- Human oversight
- Safety constraints
- Operational review
- Route audit records

---

## Education and Research Institutions

**Example Risk:** Unsafe Delegation  
An AI system is allowed to support grading, academic misconduct detection, or student decisions without proper human review.

**Possible Controls:**  
- Human review
- Appeal process
- Academic integrity policy
- Student data protection
- Decision records
